Our Mission

We protect marine biodiversity by promoting sustainable fishing practices and raising awareness. We provide fishers with interactive maps, up to date species data, and practical guides to reduce illegal fishing and preserve ecosystems.

We work hand in hand with local communities to ensure fishing remains a source of food and livelihood for generations.




Who We Are

We are university students committed to the United Nations' SDG 14 (Life Below Water). Our platform combines science, technology and education to empower communities and fishers to practice sustainable fishing, protect species and strengthen coastal livelihoods.

How to save the ocean

Why the Ocean Matters

  • Oceans cover over 70% of the planet and regulate climate and oxygen.
  • Home to extraordinary biodiversity; many species remain undiscovered.
  • Source of food and livelihood for billions.
  • Natural carbon sink: absorbs a large portion of CO₂ emissions.
  • Cultural and economic importance for coastal communities.

Challenges

Overfishing: 1/3 of global fish stocks are overexploited.

Illegal fishing: undermines sustainability and community livelihoods.

Climate & pollution: plastic, warming and acidification harm reefs.

Endangered species: many marine animals face extinction risk.
